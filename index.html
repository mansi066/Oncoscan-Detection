What is Oncoscan?

Early detection saves lives. Get screened today!
OncoScan is an AI-powered, portable cancer screening device that allows users to detect early signs of cancer anytime, anywhere. With its non-invasive technology, it ensures quick and reliable results.

How Oncoscan Works?

OncoScan leverages cutting-edge AI and advanced imaging technology to detect early signs of skin cancer and abnormalities with high accuracy. Designed for both individuals and healthcare professionals, OncoScan provides a quick, non-invasive, and AI-powered analysis of skin lesions, enabling early detection and timely medical intervention.

Frequently Asked Questions

OncoScan is an advanced AI-powered platform designed to assist in early cancer detection, risk assessment, and personalized health insights.

OncoScan analyzes medical data, symptoms, and test results to provide early indicators of potential cancer risks. It does not replace a doctor but serves as a tool for awareness and proactive health management.

OncoScan offers both free and premium features. Basic risk assessments are free, but detailed reports, AI-driven analysis, and expert consultations are part of our premium plans.
